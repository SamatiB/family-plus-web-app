FAQ

What is Family+?

A place where families connected and get together for social activities!

Why Family+ is created?

We want to connect you to other families who share the same interests and who want to meet you!

What is the age requirement for joining Family+?

You must be over 18 years old to join Family+.

How do I join Family+?

Just click on sign up button to join.

How much does it costs to join Family+?

It is free to join!

Who might we meet on Family+?

Families who Enjoy similar activities and interests. No matter if they are your next door neighbor or live around the world.

Can I add my family members to my account?

Absolutely. You can add your family members to your profile.

What if I decide that I no longer want to connect with particular family?

No problem. You can block or remove connection to previously connected families.
